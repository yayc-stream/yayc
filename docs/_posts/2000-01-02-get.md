**Latest release: v1.7.0**

#### Windows
- **Portable (ZIP)**: [yayc-v1.7.0-win-x64-portable.zip](https://github.com/yayc-stream/yayc/releases/download/yayc-v1.7.0/yayc-v1.7.0-win-x64-portable.zip)
